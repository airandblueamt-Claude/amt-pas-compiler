## Notes for QA when regenerating
- Each section starts with a divider whose page number must equal the TOC entry.
- Divider/cover/TOC carry the AMT logo header + contact-banner footer; content pages
  (rendered Excel, appended datasheets) carry their own footers.
- The sample's TOC page numbers are specific to that project's content lengths — a
  regenerated submittal will have its own page numbers, but the *structure* (cover,
  TOC, then divider-before-each-section) must match exactly.
